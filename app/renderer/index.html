Grade your own service
Recording a real service takes no special gear — pick whichever of these fits your setup.
1 Record to a USB stick on your console/mixer. Most digital mixers can record the main mix (or every channel) straight to a USB drive — start it before the service, stop it after, then drop the file in.
2 Capture on a laptop with a DAW or audio interface. Connect the board's output (or an interface) to a laptop and record into any DAW (or free tool); export a WAV/AIFF and analyze it.
3 Pull the audio from your livestream recording. If you already stream or record the service to video, extract the audio track from that file and grade it — no extra hardware at all.
